# Incident Reporting SOP

## Trigger
Report any `sev1`/`sev2` event and any event requiring authority notification.

## Required Fields
- Incident ID
- Detection timestamp (UTC)
- Affected services and airspace segments
- Safety impact summary
- Immediate mitigations
- Evidence references

## Timelines
- Initial report: within 15 minutes of classification.
- Update cadence: every 30 minutes until contained.
- Final report: within 24 hours after closure.
