# Incident Response Plan

## Scope
Covers UTM/DSS incidents affecting safety, compliance, or interoperability.

## Severity Levels
- `sev1`: immediate safety or regulatory risk.
- `sev2`: degraded service or major interoperability impact.
- `sev3`: minor service degradation.

## Response Steps
1. Detect and classify incident.
2. Activate on-call roles and assign incident commander.
3. Contain impact (safe hold/launch block/degraded mode).
4. Restore service and verify conformance.
5. Publish post-incident review with evidence links.
